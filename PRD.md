# DOTSBAR PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## Product Name

DotsBar

## Product Vision

DotsBar is the operating system for student communities, campus organizations, clubs, and associations.

The platform enables communities to manage members, host events, livestream activities, communicate effectively, and grow engagement from one centralized platform.

---

# Problem Statement

Student organizations currently manage their activities across multiple platforms:

* WhatsApp for communication
* Google Forms for registration
* Zoom for meetings
* Instagram for promotion
* Excel for member records

This creates fragmentation, poor engagement, and administrative challenges.

DotsBar provides one platform that unifies community management, event management, member engagement, and livestreaming.

---

To become the leading digital platform powering student communities across Africa and beyond.

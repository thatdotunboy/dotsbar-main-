Local Development (if you want to test locally):

Frontend: http://localhost:8000
Backend API: http://localhost:3001

Environment:
- Copy `.env.example` to `.env`
- Set `SPORTS_API_KEY` to your live sports provider key
- Optionally override `SPORTS_API_HOST`, `SPORTS_LEAGUE_ID`, and `SPORTS_SEASON`

Firebase Console: Visit https://console.firebase.google.com/project/dotsbar-6106f-2024/hosting to manage your hosting setting
